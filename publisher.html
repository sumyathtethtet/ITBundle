It is More Challenging Than Ever to Monetize

A shifting media landscape makes previous strategies outdated and/orineffective.

Traditional display advertising is under pressure.

  • Ad-blocking usage has grown 40% year-over-year
  • Organic posts on social media have diminishing reach
  • Increasing mobile traffic yields generally lower RPMs
  • Many ad networks display images that do not reflect well on the publisher brand

Traditional affiliate commerce isn’t scalable.

  • Revenue share is relatively low and inconsistent
  • Customers are lost after initial purchases
